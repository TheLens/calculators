What the proposed property tax would cost you

The proposed property tax ordinance would increase your tax bill by 7.5 mills, with 2.5 of that going to the New Orleans Fire Department and the remaining 5.0 mills going to the New Orleans Police Department.

Don't know? Visit the Orleans Parish Assessor's Office's website.

If the proposed millage passes, you would pay an additional $0.00 per year in taxes, with $0.00 of that going to the police department and $0.00 going to the fire department. This would be $0.00 in total over the 12-year lifespan of the ordinance, in current dollars.

This would increase your total tax bill from about $0 per year to $0 per year.

By Thomas Thoren | Sources: Louisiana Legislative Auditor, Orleans Parish Assessor's Office.
All numbers are in current dollars. Total tax bill numbers exclude any special taxing districts. Properties are assumed to be residential.
